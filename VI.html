PHASE VI

Phase Six of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is a group of American superhero films produced by Marvel Studios based on characters that appear in publications by Marvel Comics. Phase Six features all of the Marvel Studios productions set to be released starting from late 2024 to mid-2026, with Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures distributing the films. The first film in the phase will be Deadpool 3, scheduled for release in November 2024. Kevin Feige produces every film in this phase, alongside producer Ryan Reynolds for Deadpool 3.

At Marvel Studios' San Diego Comic-Con panel in July 2022, Feige announced that Fantastic Four would be the first film of Phase Six. He also announced that the phase would conclude with two ensemble films, Avengers: The Kang Dynasty and Avengers: Secret Wars, both to be released in 2025, and that Phase Six, along with Phase Four and Phase Five, would be part of The Multiverse Saga.[10] In September, Reynolds announced that Deadpool 3 would be released on September 6, 2024,[11] as the first film in Phase Six.[12] In October, Marvel Studios delayed the Phase Five film Blade to the September 2024 slot, and pushed back the releases of Deadpool 3, Fantastic Four, and Avengers: Secret Wars as a result.[13]
